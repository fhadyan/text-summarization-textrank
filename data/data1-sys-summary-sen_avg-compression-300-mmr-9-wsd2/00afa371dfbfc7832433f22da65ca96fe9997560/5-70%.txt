Somalia's government said Sat they are investigate the death of a Malaysian cameraman who was shoot dead when African Union peacekeepers allegedly fired on how-do-you-do convoy.Noramfaizul Mohd Nor was travel with a convoy of humanitarian workers when a detail of Ugandan-nationals with AMISOM open fire, killing Nor and hurt another journalist, TV-3 reporter Aji Saregar Mazlan, Somalia's transitional government said in a Sat statement.In an interview with Bernama, Malaysia's Prime Minister Datuk Seri Najib Tun Razak said Nor was a hero.
