Phnom Penh, Cambodia The cause of a mysterious illness that has claimed the lives of more than 60 Cambodian children has been determined, medical doctors familiar with the investigation told CNN on Wednesday A combination of pathogens, disease-causing micro-organisms, is to blame for the illness, the World Health Organization, in conjunction with the Cambodian Ministry of Health, has concluded, the doctors said The inappropriate use of steroids, which can suppress the immune system, worsened the illness in a majority of the patients, the doctors said Parents face anxious wait over mystery illness
