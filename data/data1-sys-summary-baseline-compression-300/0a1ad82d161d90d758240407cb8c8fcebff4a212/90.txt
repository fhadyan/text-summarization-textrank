BEIJING, China Soon after I first came to visit China in the autumn of 1971, I saw a contingent of militia soldiers doing marching drills in Tiananmen Square  I was told they were rehearsing for the annual National Day parade on October 1, which people eagerly awaited Mao Zedong proclaims the founding of the People's Republic of China in Beijing on October 1, 1949 Weeks later, however, I was informed that the civilian and military parade had been cancelled in the spirit of simple-living and hard struggle, as Chairman Mao decreed  To rebel is justified! they proclaimed
