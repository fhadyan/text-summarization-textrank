 Miami is well known for its tropical weather, retiree community and Cuban-American population, but not so much as an international art hub  Luckily for downtown Miami, billionaire real-estate developer Jorge Pérez wants to change that The real estate developer and art lover donated $40 million, half made up from his private art collection, to the Pérez Art Museum Miami, known as PAMM, one of the few major institutions in the United States with a Hispanic name That's certainly one way to put Miami on the map
