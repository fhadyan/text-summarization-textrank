Super Bowl XLVIII started out bad for the Denver Broncos.
  Manning was thwarted in his quest for a second Super Bowl ring.
 Denver, led by the 37-year-old Manning, has the NFL's best offense, statistically, but never got moving Sunday.
 Seattle's defense was so strong that Denver managed only 27 yards rushing, compared with 135 for Seattle.
 Fittingly, a Seattle defensive player, linebacker Malcolm Smith, was named the game's most valuable player.
