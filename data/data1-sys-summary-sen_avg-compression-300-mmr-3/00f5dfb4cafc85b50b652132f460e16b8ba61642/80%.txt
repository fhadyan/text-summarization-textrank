Paris Investigators trying to determine why an Air France plane crashed mysteriously two years ago have recovered the complete contents of the flight data recorder and the last two hours of cockpit conversation, they announced Monday It will take several weeks to analyze the data, French air accident experts said Investigators also brought an engine and an avionics bay containing computers to the surface, they said The fuselage was discovered in April with bodies still inside, investigators said CNN's Ayesha Durgahee, Catherine Clifford and Niki Cook contributed to this report
