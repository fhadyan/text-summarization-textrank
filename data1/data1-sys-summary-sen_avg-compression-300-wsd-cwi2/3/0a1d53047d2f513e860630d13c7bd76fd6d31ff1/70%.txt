London Work on the cross's largest solar bridge has started in central LondonThe new solar roof spanning Blackfriars Railway Bridge above the River Thames will cover more than 6,000 square meters when finished, according to developersThe solar roof and the new station are due to be completed by the summer of 2012 Blackfriars Bridge is an ideal location for solar, Derry Newman, chief executive of Solar Century, said in a statement.
