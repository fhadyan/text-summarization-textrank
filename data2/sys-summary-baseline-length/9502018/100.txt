 In this paper we describe a method for analysing the temporal structure of a discourse.  This component was implemented as part of a discourse grammar for English.  The goals of the temporal component were to yield a detailed representation of the temporal structure of the discourse, taking into account the effect of tense, aspect and temporal expressions while at the same time minimising unnecessary ambiguity in the temporal structure.
