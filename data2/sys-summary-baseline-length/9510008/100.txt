 Recently, RD efforts involving machine translation of different language families, such as Japanese and English, have become popular , , .  However, differences in perspective and how objects are thought of, in such different language families, affect how expressions are structured.  These differences in expression structure make it difficult to convert from one language to another mechanically.  As a means of solving this problem, efforts have been made in the area of sub-languages or knowledge-based translation .  This action of re-writing is normally known as pre-editing .
