 Several methods have been proposed to deal with anaphora resolution and prepositional phrase (PP) attachment phenomenon and separately, so that the literature is very abundant : for PPs see e.g (Frazier and Fodor, 1979; Hobbs, 1990; Wilks and Huang, 1985 and for anaphora see e.g (Carter, 1986; Reinhart, 1983; Sidner, 1983   However none of these methods has considered the way of dealing both phenomena in the same concrete system.  The disambiguation procedure aims at filling the empty roles using attachment rules.
