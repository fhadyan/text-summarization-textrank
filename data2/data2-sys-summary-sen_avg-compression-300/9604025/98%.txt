 In a cascading word- POS guesser is described It applies first morphological prefix and suffix guessing rules and then ending-guessing rules There are two kinds of word-guessing rules employed by the cascading guesser: morphological rules and ending guessing rules Morphological word-guessing rules describe how one word can be guessed given that another word is known Unlike morphological guessing rules, ending-guessing rules do not require the main form of an unknown word to be listed in the lexicon they are able to cover less unknown words
