 Each noun phrase was given one of the following scores: STRUCTURE: problem with structure or choice of translation BEST: the most appropriate article/number ARTICLE: inappropriate article NUMBER: inappropriate number POSSESSIVE: inappropriate use of possessive determiner COUNTABILITY: problem with countability REFERENCE: problem with referential property For the purpose of evaluating the generation of articles and number, noun phrases that were either the BEST possible translation, or that had a problem only with STRUCTURE/CHOICE OF TRANSLATION, were judged to be successful..
