 Emergence of text categorization according to language came with the need of processing texts coming from all over the world.  We remark that a change of language in a text could appear at each change of sentence (more often paragraph) or in each included segment via quotes, parenthesis, dashes or colons.  Grammatical words are proper to each language and are in a whole different from one language to another.  To manage the possible points of change of language via included segments (see section the language classification procedure uses a recursive algorithm to easily handle changes of context.
